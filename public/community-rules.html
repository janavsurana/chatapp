🏘️ Community Rules

Chat is a community of developers, gamers, and creators. To keep it safe and welcoming for everyone, please follow these rules.

📢 No Spam
  • No repetitive messages or flooding the chat
  • No mass promotional content or unsolicited advertising
  • No botting, scripting, or automated spam
  • No excessive caps lock or symbols
⚠️ First offense: Warning | Repeat: 24-hour mute | Persistent: Ban
🚫 No Scams or Fraud
  • No phishing, catfishing, or identity theft
  • No fake giveaways or pump-and-dump schemes
  • No selling counterfeit or stolen items
  • No financial scams or MLM recruitment
⚠️ First offense: Immediate 30-day ban | Repeat: Permanent ban + potential legal action
❤️ Respect & Inclusion
  • No slurs, hate speech, or discrimination
  • No racism, sexism, homophobia, or transphobia
  • No harassment, bullying, or personal attacks
  • No doxxing or sharing private information
⚠️ First offense: 7-day ban | Repeat: 30-day ban | Persistent: Permanent ban
🔞 Content & Appropriateness
  • Chat is 18+ only. No exceptions.
  • No explicit sexual content, gore, or violence
  • No child safety concerns or inappropriate content involving minors
  • No illegal content (piracy, drugs, weapons)
⚠️ First offense: Immediate ban + account deletion + potential report to authorities
🏪 Marketplace Ethics (Coming Soon)
  • Be honest about product descriptions and quality
  • No inflated prices or artificial scarcity
  • Deliver digital assets as promised
  • Honor refund policies and disputes
⚠️ Repeated violations: Seller badge removed + account suspension
🔧 Platform Integrity
  • No hacking, exploiting bugs, or DDoS attempts
  • No account farming or multi-accounting for manipulation
  • No scraping user data or API abuse
  • Report security vulnerabilities responsibly
⚠️ First offense: Account termination | Legal action for severe breaches
✅ What We Love to See
  • Share your projects and get feedback
  • Help others solve problems
  • Collaborate and make friends
  • Respect different opinions and backgrounds
  • Report rule violations instead of taking action yourself
⚖️ Enforcement & Appeals

Our moderation team reviews reports and enforces rules fairly. We believe in second chances but take safety seriously.

If you think a moderation decision is unfair: Reply to the notification with your appeal within 7 days. We'll review it within 48 hours.

❓ Questions?

Check our FAQ or reach out in the Feedback & Help room. Our community managers are here to help!
