Refund Policy

1. Creator Subscriptions

Creator subscriptions ($5/month) are eligible for refunds within 24 hours of purchase. After 24 hours, subscriptions are non-refundable. Refunds are automatically approved within 3-5 business days.

2. Product Purchases

Digital products (code, assets, templates) sold by creators are non-refundable. Creators are responsible for their product descriptions and quality. If a product does not match its description, contact the creator directly for resolution.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund within 24 hours:

  • Log into your account
  • Go to your subscription settings
  • Click "Request Refund"
  • Confirm your request
  • Refund will be processed automatically

4. Payment Methods

Refunds are issued to the original payment method used:

  • Crypto payments: Refunded to the wallet address used
  • Credit/Debit card: Refunded to the card used
  • Refunds may take 3-7 business days to appear

5. Non-Refundable Items

The following are NOT eligible for refunds:

  • Creator products (after purchase)
  • Subscriptions purchased more than 24 hours ago
  • Promotional or discounted subscriptions
  • Refunds requested for fraudulent transactions (will be investigated)

6. Refund Disputes

If you believe a refund was incorrectly denied or processed, you may contact us with:

  • Your username and account email
  • Transaction ID or date of purchase
  • Reason for dispute
  • Screenshots or evidence

7. Chargeback Policy

If you initiate a chargeback or dispute through your payment provider, we will:

  • Investigate the claim
  • Provide evidence of the transaction
  • Permanently ban your account if the chargeback is found to be fraudulent

8. Subscription Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. No refunds are issued for partial months.

9. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting.
