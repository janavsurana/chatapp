Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Chat platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Username, password (hashed), email (optional)
  • Messages: Text content of messages you send in chat rooms
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the platform (rooms visited, message timestamps)
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the chat platform
  • Verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • Send transactional messages (account notifications)
  • Monitor and improve platform performance
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (not stored in plain text)
  • All connections use HTTPS encryption
  • Data is stored in secure databases
  • Access is restricted to authorized personnel

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Messages: Retained indefinitely unless you request deletion
  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data deleted within 30 days of account deletion request

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Cloud database provider
  • Render: Hosting provider
  • NOWPayments: Payment processor
  • OpenAI: Content moderation

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of promotional communications

8. Cookies

We do not use cookies. Authentication is managed through secure tokens stored in your browser's local storage.

9. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover a child has provided information, we will delete it immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Your continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of changes.
