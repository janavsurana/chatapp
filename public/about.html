About Us

What is Chat?

Chat is a pseudonymous creator marketplace built for programmers and game developers to connect, collaborate, and monetize their skills. We provide a secure, private platform where creators can sell code, assets, templates, and expertise to a global community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower independent creators by providing them with a simple, secure, and fair platform to build sustainable income. We believe in privacy, transparency, and creator freedom.

Why We Built Chat

Traditional platforms charge high fees, lock creators into complicated contracts, and don't respect privacy. We created Chat to be different:

  • Low fees: Only 10% commission on sales (creators keep 90%)
  • Privacy first: No personal documents required, completely pseudonymous
  • Creator friendly: Simple pricing, instant payouts, full control
  • Global payments: Crypto payments accepted worldwide
  • Fair moderation: AI-powered, transparent, human-reviewed when needed

Who We Are

Chat was founded by a solo indie developer with a passion for creator economy and decentralized finance. We're bootstrapped, independent, and committed to building something that actually helps creators.

Our Values

🔐 Privacy

We respect your privacy. No Aadhaar cards, no government ID required. You control your data.

⚡ Simplicity

We keep things simple. No confusing pricing, no hidden fees, no corporate jargon.

🛡️ Security

Your account and transactions are protected with industry-standard encryption and security practices.

🌍 Global

We serve creators worldwide. No geographic restrictions. Crypto payments mean no banking barriers.

💪 Creator First

Every feature we build is for creators. Your success is our success.
